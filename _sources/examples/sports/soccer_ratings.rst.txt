Rating/Ranking Soccer Teams Example
===================================
This example concerns the first 20 matches that took place during the 2018 - 2019 sport season of English Premier League.
The following rating methods are used to rate and rank teams:

   notebooks/soccer_accurate

The teams were ordered alphabetically in the rating and ranking results of each method.
