Rating/Ranking Soccer Teams Example

This example concerns the first 20 matches that took place during the 2018 - 2019 sport season of English Premier League. The following rating methods are used to rate and rank teams:

  • WinLoss Example
  • Colley Example
  • Massey Example
  • Elo-Win Example
  • Elo-Point Example
  • Offense Defense Example
  • AccuRATE Example
  • GeM (Markov) Example

The teams were ordered alphabetically in the rating and ranking results of each method.
